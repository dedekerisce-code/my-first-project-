Author Spotlights

Dive into the lives and works of some of the most influential authors in literature.

Jane Austen

English novelist known for Pride and Prejudice and Sense and Sensibility. A master of wit and social commentary.

George Orwell

Author of 1984 and Animal Farm. Known for his sharp political writing and dystopian fiction.

J.K. Rowling

Creator of the Harry Potter series. One of the best-selling authors of all time.

Ernest Hemingway

Nobel Prize winner known for The Old Man and the Sea and A Farewell to Arms.

Toni Morrison

Nobel Prize winning author of Beloved. A powerful voice in American literature.

Frank Herbert

Author of the legendary Dune series. A pioneer of science fiction worldbuilding.
